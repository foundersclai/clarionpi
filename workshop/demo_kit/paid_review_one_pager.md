on a small set of files and tell us where it is wrong, thin, or unsafe.

offer of ownership, partnership, or unpaid advisory work, and it is **not** an endorsement — we want
candid, critical review, not a testimonial.

- You review a generated demand package: the letter, the chronology, the exhibit provenance, and the
  deadline handling.

- You are paid for your time at your stated hourly rate; the deliverable is a written review memo.

**What this is not:** not a request to put real client data into the demonstration, not a
solicitation of clients, and no promises about outcomes or results from the software.

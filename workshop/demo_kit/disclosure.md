## Why a slide instead of in-product labels

In the shipping product, demo/pilot identity and evidence labeling are enforced by the software
itself (the **S13** labeling controls). For this operator-led session we substitute this spoken +
slide disclosure for those product-enforced labels. That is a deliberate judgment call, and it holds
**only because attendees never operate the product**: the operator drives, the database is
disposable, the data is synthetic, and no attendee or client information is entered. The moment
anyone other than the operator drives the product — or any real matter is involved — the S13
in-product labeling is required and this spoken substitute no longer applies.

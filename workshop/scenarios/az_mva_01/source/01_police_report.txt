NARRATIVE
Unit 1 was stopped for a red signal in the northbound curb lane of N. 7th
Street at E. Camelback Road. Unit 2, traveling northbound in the same lane,
failed to stop and struck Unit 1 in the rear. Unit 1 was pushed forward
approximately one vehicle length. Both drivers remained on scene.

Driver of Unit 2 stated he "looked down for a moment" before impact.
Independent witness (statement attached to case file) corroborated that Unit 1
was fully stopped at the signal.

CONTRIBUTING FACTORS / CITATION
Unit 2 driver cited under A.R.S. 28-730 (following too closely). No citation
issued to Unit 1. This is a collision between two private motorists; no
government or public-entity vehicle was involved.

INJURY / DISPOSITION
Unit 1 driver reported neck and lower-back pain and right-shoulder pain at the
scene; transported by private vehicle to Saguaro Regional Medical Center
emergency department. Unit 2 driver reported no injury.
